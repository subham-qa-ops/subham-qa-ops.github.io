The Automation Ecosystem

01 | CSG Command Center

The Challenge Leadership lacked a centralized, real-time view of CSG operations. Data was siloed across multiple spreadsheets, causing high reporting latency and missed coaching opportunities.
The Solution Engineered an enterprise-grade Looker Studio BI dashboard that autonomously aggregates thousands of backend data points into interactive, drill-down visualizations.
Business Impact Transforms raw numbers into strategic storytelling, enabling leadership to make instant, data-driven decisions regarding capacity planning and floor-level performance.
Looker Studio Data Visualization

02 | Process Knowledge Hub

The Challenge Process deviations are the top drivers of errors across core metrics (CSAT, EQ, FDR, TDR). Critical updates were getting lost in crowded email inboxes, allowing agents to claim ignorance.
The Solution Engineered a centralized, mandatory web portal where agents must log in to read updates, access KB articles, and digitally "Acknowledge" receipt of new procedures.
Business Impact Creates a rigid, timestamped audit trail, entirely eliminating communication gaps, mitigating compliance risks, and ensuring 100% visibility of new processes.
Web App UI/UX Compliance

03 | QA Huddle Dispatcher

The Challenge Quality Analysts wasted extensive time manually preparing daily discussion pointers, and agents absent during huddles repeatedly missed critical process updates.
The Solution Developed an automated portal that pulls targeted errors, syncs attendance from Google Sheets, dispatches daily summaries, and saves to a searchable digital archive.
Business Impact Recaptures significant QA prep time, standardizes coaching across all shifts, and provides a continuous learning hub for agents to self-serve missed huddle insights.
Web App Workflow Automation

04 | Backlog Audit & SLA Tracker

The Challenge Backlog cases were aging due to a lack of immediate accountability. Quality errors in tickets lacked a strict, trackable timeline for agents to execute corrections.
The Solution Built a high-velocity, closed-loop web application that audits cases, dispatches specific corrective actions, and strictly enforces a 5-hour resolution SLA.
Business Impact Directly drives down overall backlog volume and aging by ensuring "One-Touch" rapid corrections with automated escalation triggers to leadership.
JavaScript Google Apps Script HTML/CSS

05 | Governance & Reporting Alerts

The Challenge Chasing agents and managers to acknowledge quality feedback relied entirely on human memory and manual follow-ups, causing severe delays in the coaching lifecycle.
The Solution Designed an automated accountability workflow for audits. When an audit is published, an automated email is dispatched, establishing a strict 48-Hour Acknowledgment SLA.
Business Impact Enforces rigid operational governance without QA manual intervention. Unacknowledged feedback autonomously triggers Escalation Emails directly to leadership.
Server-side Triggers Workflow Architecture

06 | Intelligent Data Parsers

The Challenge The QA team wasted 45-60 minutes daily downloading CSVs, mapping agent rosters, calculating complex resolution metrics manually, and collating DSAT/EQ scores from emails.
The Solution Built a backend processing engine that autonomously scans system reports and email traffic, instantly mapping data to the roster and writing it to the master database.
Business Impact Eliminates human calculation errors entirely with 100% precision. Recaptures 40+ hours monthly and enables "Golden Hour" service recovery for negative DSATs.
